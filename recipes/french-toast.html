French Toast Recipe

Description

This fabulous French toast recipe works with many types of bread â€” white, whole wheat, brioche, cinnamon-raisin, Italian, or French! Delicious served hot with butter and maple syrup.

Ingredients

  • 6 Slices White Bread
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2/3 Cup Milk
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • 1 Tablespoon Butter

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.

  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.

  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat.

  4. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.

  5. Fold the bottom of each tortilla up about 2 inches, and start rolling from the right side. When wrap is halfway rolled, fold the top of the tortilla down, enclosing the filling, and continue rolling to make a tight, compact cylinder.

  6. Serve hot.
